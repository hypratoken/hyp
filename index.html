About HYP

HYP is a decentralized BEP-20 token on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), launched with the goal of creating a fair and secure crypto asset aligned with Islamic values. It features anti-whale, auto-burn, and cooldown systems to prevent abuse and manipulation.

Tokenomics

  • Buy Tax: 0.5%
